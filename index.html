World Largest railway bridge

The world's largest railway bridge, in terms of height above the riverbed, is the Chenab Railway Bridge in Jammu and Kashmir, India. It stands 359 meters (1,178 feet) above the Chenab River, making it 35 meters taller than the Eiffel Tower according to the Hindu. The bridge is a single-arch railway bridge and was inaugurated on June 10, 2025.

Published by THG(The hindustan gazette)

How do political parties understand gender and women

Indian political parties are increasingly targeting women voters with various promises, acknowledging their growing electoral participation despite often limited political awareness. While parties offer benefits to secure their votes, crucial issues like domestic violence and safety are often neglected. The article emphasizes women's need to leverage their voting power to demand accountability and ensure their rights.
